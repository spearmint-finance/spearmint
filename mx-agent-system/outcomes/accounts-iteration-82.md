# Accounts Iteration 82 — Account Search Bar

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**PR:** #229
**Focus:** Add search bar to accounts page for filtering by name or institution

## Before
- No way to search/filter accounts on the accounts page
- Users with many accounts had to scroll through all cards

## After
- Search bar appears when there are more than 3 accounts
- Filters cards by account name or institution name (case-insensitive)
- Clear button to reset search
- Tab counts update to reflect filtered results

## Outcome
- Human intervention: No
- Measurable: Yes — accounts searchable by name/institution
- Regressions: None
