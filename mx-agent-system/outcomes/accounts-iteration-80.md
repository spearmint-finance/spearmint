# Accounts Iteration 80 — Inactive Account Visual Indicator on Cards

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**PR:** #221
**Focus:** Show inactive accounts with reduced opacity and warning chip on the accounts page

## Before
- Inactive accounts looked identical to active accounts on the accounts page
- No way to tell at a glance which accounts were inactive

## After
- Inactive account cards have reduced opacity (0.6)
- "Inactive" warning chip shown next to account type chip

## Outcome
- Human intervention: No
- Measurable: Yes — inactive accounts visually distinguishable
- Regressions: None
